ABOUT NURSING TRAINING FORMS
  1. All purchased vouchers are available for use up to the deadline, if not used

  2. No postage envelopes are required, communication is mainly via sms or email

  3. Have a dedicated phone number and a personal email address for all correspondence

  4. You will need your residential and Ghana Post Digital Address for the online application

  5. The online registration form is accessible only by the PIN and self-created password

  6. Only Applicants who meet admission requirements will be invited to attend a competitive interview at the school of choice

  7. Wrong filling of the form will affect your admission

HOW TO FILL NURSING TRAINING FORMS
  1. Begin the application process by purchasing application codes from any Agricultural Development Bank (ADB) branch, Ghana Commercial Bank (GCB) branch or using the short code.

  2. After making payment at the bank, you will be given a voucher with a unique PIN and SERIAL Number.

  3. Visit Application form to access the online application form.

  4. Enter the PIN code and SERIAL number given to go at the bank to begin filling the Nursing And Midwifery Training College Application Form Online

  5. Follow the detailed instructions carefully and fill the relevant stages of the admission form

  6. After a successful submission of the completed Nursing And Midwifery Training College Online Form, you can use the PIN and SERIAL Number to track your admission status
